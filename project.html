With the rapid development of the Internet, the labor crowdsourcing model based on mobile platforms gathers the power of the public to ensure the authenticity of survey data. By setting up a reasonable remuneration for tasks, the company can make greater profits.

By consulting related literature, we found that although the crowdsourcing model in the Internet age is developing hotly, many large enterprises and companies have developed through the power of netizens, but due to the irrationality of task remuneration, enterprise development is at risk, so according to the users themselves Information and specific task information to formulate a reasonable task pricing plan is imminent. This project focuses on the issue of using the power of netizens to conduct market surveys, and formulates remuneration for different tasks based on crowdsourcing theory.

The essence of "making money by taking pictures" through APP is the completion of the big task of market research through the collaboration of users. Since each task and each user has a different geographic location, the task remuneration is determined according to the completion of each small task and the member information.
